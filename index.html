Sehi L’Yi
Hi! I'm a PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University and a member of Human-Computer Interaction Lab, advised by Jinwook Seo.

In Information Visualization (InfoVis) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) fields, my research interest is helping people to understand their complex data with visualizations and interactive user interfaces. I designed and implemented visual analytics systems for bioinformaticians and worked on understandable visualization recommendation interfaces.
